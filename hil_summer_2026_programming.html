Founder Fundamentals Validate your idea — customer discovery, experimentation, early skill-building
Entry: supported activation ▾

⚙️ Delivery mechanisms

  • Primer — async, self-paced email journey for solo exploration
  • Sprint — sequenced cohort: kickoff → field work → check-in → showcase
  • Hackathon — two-part: build prototype → return with customer feedback
  • Themed Office hours — drop-in peer + advisor facilitation

📚 Content this summer

  • Customer discovery — interviews,assumption testing,validation
  • AI agent development — B2C concept-to-test with AI tools

👥 Who it's for

  • Pre-product and idea-stage founders
  • Students actively exploring a venture
  • Founders looking for accountability
  • Founders able to commit to 1-3 hrs/wk of work
  • Primer requires commitment to async, self-activation
  • Sprint requires commitment to 3 week series

Entry model — supported activation

Open access, but founders may need guidance to find the right starting point (Primer vs. Sprint).

User Journey

Discover (website / staff) → Register for right program → Activate → Engage in content → Keep warm if disengaged
