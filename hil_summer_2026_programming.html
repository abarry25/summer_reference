How to Navigate

By Founder Need

Start with a founder's challenge or goal → identify the right container → review delivery mechanisms.

Questions to ask founders

  • What stage are you at?
  • Do you have a product yet?
  • Are you looking for a cohort or self-paced support?
  • How many hours a week can you commit?
  • Have you heard of or participated in any of our programs before?
  • Are you looking for something structured or flexible?
  • Do you need funding, community, or curriculum?
For Internal Use

Start with an operational, technical, or marketing need → identify the Program Lead → review entry and delivery model.

Internal questions you might have

  • Who do I coordinate with on an upcoming program?
  • Will participants need member login access for a program?
  • What's the difference between Venture Incubation and Acceleration?
  • Where will Program Staff be focused this summer?

⚠️ Important: This reference describes system and structure — not day-to-day schedules. Specific dates and deadlines should be confirmed through current planning resources.
