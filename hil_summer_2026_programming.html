🌱
Founder Fundamentals Validate your idea — customer discovery, experimentation, early skill-building
Entry: intentional activation ▾

⚙️ Delivery mechanisms

  • Primer — async, self-paced email journey
  • Sprint — kickoff → field work → check-in → showcase
  • Hackathon — two-part: build prototype → return with results
  • Office hours — drop-in peer + advisor facilitation

📚 Content this summer

  • Customer discovery — interviews,assumption testing,validation
  • AI agent development — B2C concept-to-test with AI tools

👥 Who it's for

  • Pre-product and idea-stage founders
  • Students actively exploring a venture
  • Open membership — requires sign-up or sprint registration
  • Primer and Sprint serve different commitment levels

Entry model — intentional activation

Open access, but founders need guidance to find the right starting point (Primer vs. Sprint). The team helps members activate and maintains a keep-warm loop for those who disengage early.

Discover (website / staff) → Register for right program → Activate (kickoff / first email) → Engage in content → Keep warm if disengaged
🏛️
Venture Incubation Milestone-gated progression — pitches, advancement, advisor accountability
Entry: intentional activation + selection ▾

⚙️ Delivery mechanisms

  • Application pipeline — structured review, interview, and selection process
  • Milestone check-ins — regular advisor meetings tied to venture progress gates
  • Internal pitches — advancement pitches to panel at milestone junctions
  • Advancement ceremonies — cohort recognition events open to all members

📚 Content this summer

  • Venture milestones — what progress looks like at each stage of the VIP
  • Personalized advisor feedback — content shaped by each venture's specific needs
  • Advancement Ceremony — open to all members, celebration
  • Advancement Panel — pitch panel

👥 Who it's for

  • Selected cohort members
  • Founders actively working toward defined venture milestones
  • People ready for focused program commitment

Entry model — intentional activation + selection

Requires application, review, personal interview, and payment. The team actively manages every step. There is an explicit "not yet" path with re-entry options for founders who aren't selected.

Application → Review + interview → Selection → Onboarding + orientation → Milestone progression
🚀
Acceleration Cohort-based company building — workshops, clinics, lunches, pitch event
Entry: intentional activation + selection ▾

⚙️ Delivery mechanisms

  • Workshops — weekly virtual sessions, cohort-only curriculum
  • Clinics — targeted deep-dives on specific venture challenges
  • Board meeting check-ins — structured advisor accountability touchpoints
  • Community lunches — weekly in-person gathering
  • Pitch event — 5-day jury pitch

📚 Content this summer

  • Company building curriculum — weekly workshop topics across 6+ sessions
  • Fundraising + growth — embedded throughout cohort programming
  • Pitch preparation — cumulative, building toward jury event
  • Peer learning — alumni + student founders in same cohort

👥 Who it's for

  • Summer Accelerator cohort — alumni and student founders
  • Founders pursuing the LLX (Launch Lab X) pathway
  • Highest-intensity container in the summer set
  • Led by Joanna Furgiuele + Rym Baouendi

Entry model — intentional activation + selection

Cohort selected before summer begins. Orientation launches the experience. Weekly rhythm creates accountability. Culminates in a jury pitch that determines LLX advancement — the highest-stakes gate in summer programming.

Selection + orientation → Weekly workshops + lunches → Board meeting check-ins → Pitch event → LLX decision

Venture Growth Expert talks, accelerator pathways, open workshops — register and show up
Entry: minimal activation ▾

⚙️ Delivery mechanisms

  • Founder talks — expert-led lunch conversations + live Q&A
  • Accelerator info sessions — direct pipeline to YC and Techstars
  • Open workshops — single-session, skill-based

📚 Content this summer

  • Fundraising — Allison Byers
  • Accelerator Info Sessions — YC + Techstars sessions
  • AI for communications — PRStack toolkit
  • MVP feedback — peer product testing
  • Scaling an AI startup — Brian Elliott + Murph Vandervelde (Blitzy)

👥 Who it's for

  • Open membership — all member types welcome
  • Best fit for post-idea, post-MVP founders
  • No prior program participation required
  • Highest reach, lowest operational overhead of any container

Entry model — minimal activation

Register and show up. No follow-up cadence required from the team. Each event is self-contained — there's no arc to track. The team's job is logistics and marketing, not member management.

See event (website / email) → Register → Attend

Open Community Social events, peer circles, co-founder connections — the glue of the summer
Entry: minimal activation ▾

⚙️ Delivery mechanisms

  • Socials — Member Appreciation, Summer Launch Party at Felipe's
  • Pitch — Founder/Joiner Pizza + Pitch
  • Peer circles — Founder Circles in-person + virtual
  • Problem-solving — structured peer exchange, no prep needed

📚 Content this summer

  • Peer learning — content emerges from the room, not a curriculum
  • Network building — co-founder matching, collaborator + joiner connections
  • Community identity — belonging, shared experience, i-lab culture
  • Ashley Olafsen leads all community events

👥 Who it's for

  • All members, all stages, all programs
  • Life Lab residents, LLX, Climate Circle cohorts
  • Solo founders actively seeking co-founders or collaborators
  • Anyone who just wants to be in the room

Entry model — minimal activation

Register and show up. The value is in the room itself — peer energy, serendipitous connections, informal conversation. No follow-up needed. This container is the connective tissue between all other containers.

See event (website / email) → Register → Attend
